• Each player is dealt 13 cards. To show and get minimum score (0), Assemble all 13 cards based on...
› Create 2 or more sequences of 3 or 4 cards (same suite)
› other cards can be assembled into triplets of 3 or 4 cards (all cards are same number of different suite)
• Minimum number of sequences to avoid full count = 2
› 1 original sequence & 1 non-original sequence w/ a joker is allowed
› 2 non-original sequences are allowed when player has 2 jokers
› Unless all triplets without joker is showed by a player
